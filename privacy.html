Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 14, 2025

1. Information Collection

Video Automation Workflow only collects and processes the minimum data necessary for operation:

  • TikTok username and user ID (for authentication)
  • Access tokens (stored securely, never shared)
  • Video metadata (title, description, hashtags)

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account
  • Upload videos on your behalf
  • Process video metadata according to your workflow configuration

3. Data Storage and Security

All authentication tokens are:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Encrypted using industry-standard methods
  • Never transmitted to third-party servers
  • Never shared with other users or services

4. Third-Party Services

This application integrates with:

  • TikTok API: For video uploads (governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy)
  • No other third-party services collect or process your data

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

  • Access tokens: Until you revoke authorization
  • Video metadata: Only during upload process
  • No long-term data storage or logging

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access at any time through TikTok settings
  • Request deletion of all stored data
  • Access information about data we process

7. Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date will reflect any changes.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, visit our GitHub repository.

10. GDPR Compliance

For EU users, we comply with GDPR requirements:

  • Legal basis for processing: Consent (through TikTok OAuth)
  • Data controller: Independent developer (see GitHub)
  • Data processor: TikTok (for API operations)
  • Right to data portability: Available on request
